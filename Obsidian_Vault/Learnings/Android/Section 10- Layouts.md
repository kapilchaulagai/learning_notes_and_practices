		- align
		- position
		- margin / padding

79. **Constraint Layout** ![[android-constraint-layout.png]]
	- Has Anchors to add constraints
	- Responsive
	- Dynamic
	- Can Achieve Complex Layout
	- Reduces Nested Layouts
